# Customer Feedback Executive Report

## 1. Executive Overview
This report synthesizes recent customer feedback to provide a high-level view of our current service landscape. While pockets of excellence exist, the overall tone is one of frustration, particularly among long-term clients who feel their loyalty is being overlooked.

*   **Bright Spots:** Branches in the **BALI_NUSA and JAVA regions** (specifically BR-BALI-003 and BR-JAVA-006) are delivering exceptional, human-centric service that is successfully building brand loyalty.
*   **Systemic Risks:** Our digital infrastructure is currently a major pain point, with frequent outages and poor navigation causing customers to abandon digital tools in favor of branch visits.
*   **Operational Bottlenecks:** Lending and mortgage processes are suffering from severe, unexplained delays, leading to significant customer anxiety and potential loss of business.
*   **Security Concerns:** Fraud detection and resolution are failing to meet expectations, with customers reporting long wait times for support and a lack of transparency during investigations.
*   **Loyalty Erosion:** A recurring theme across all negative clusters is the feeling that long-term customers are receiving generic, unhelpful responses, which is driving them to consider switching to competitors.

---

## 2. Cluster-by-Cluster Insight

### Card And Rewards (16 docs)
*   **Debit Card Logistics:** Customers are frustrated by multi-week delays in card replacements and a lack of proactive shipping updates.
*   **Rewards Transparency:** Users report points-accrual errors and feel that recent changes to the rewards program constitute a "hidden devaluation" of their benefits.

### Excellent Branch Service (56 docs)
*   **Service Excellence:** Staff at specific locations are highly praised for their patience, professionalism, and ability to explain complex financial products clearly.
*   **Digital Integration:** Customers in these regions appreciate the balance of modern digital tools (kiosks, tablets) with high-quality, in-person support.

### Digital Platform Instability (56 docs)
*   **System Outages:** Frequent login failures and app crashes are preventing customers from performing basic tasks like fund transfers.
*   **UX Friction:** Recent updates have made the interface cluttered, burying essential features and increasing the time required for simple banking activities.

### Lending Process Delays (49 docs)
*   **Administrative Stagnation:** Mortgage and loan applications are experiencing extreme, unexplained delays, with files often appearing "stuck" in the system.
*   **Communication Gaps:** Customers report being passed between departments with no clear accountability or status updates, leading to a loss of trust.

### Fraud Security Concerns (16 docs)
*   **Detection Failures:** Customers are reporting unauthorized transactions that were not flagged by our automated systems.
*   **Resolution Frustration:** The investigation process is perceived as opaque, with long hold times and a lack of clear case tracking or human support.

### Billing Fee Issues (45 docs)
*   **Unexpected Charges:** Customers are reporting unauthorized maintenance or overdraft fees, often contradicting the terms agreed upon at account opening.
*   **System Errors:** Technical glitches, such as double-billing or incorrect overdraft triggers, are causing significant distress.

### Branch Tablet Failures (12 docs)
*   **Hardware/Software Issues:** In-branch tablets are frequently described as laggy, unresponsive, or confusing, which forces customers back into manual teller queues.

### Unprofessional Branch Staff (15 docs)
*   **Service Standards:** Reports of dismissive or condescending behavior at specific branches (BR-BALI-017 and BR-JAVA-006) are damaging the bank's reputation.
*   **Operational Neglect:** Understaffing and poor queue management are leading to wait times of up to two hours.

### Long Wait Times (38 docs)
*   **Resource Management:** Customers observe empty desks during peak hours, suggesting a mismatch between staffing levels and customer traffic.
*   **Process Inefficiency:** Redundant documentation requirements and poor internal coordination are forcing customers to visit branches for issues that should be resolvable digitally.

---

## 3. Cross-Cutting Themes
*   **The "Digital-to-Branch" Migration:** When our digital platforms fail, customers are forced into branches. This creates a "double-negative" experience: they are already frustrated by the app, and then they encounter long wait times or broken tablets in the lobby.
*   **The Loyalty Gap:** A consistent thread across all negative clusters is that long-term customers feel their tenure is ignored. They expect a higher level of personalized service and are deeply disappointed when met with generic scripts or automated responses.
*   **Communication Transparency:** Whether it is a loan application, a fraud investigation, or a system outage, customers feel they are being kept in the dark. The lack of proactive, honest communication is a primary driver of the desire to switch providers.
